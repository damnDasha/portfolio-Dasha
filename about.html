About Me

None of those jobs have brought me as much joy and excitement as coding does. I know I'm only getting started, but spending hours on my Javascript apps has been the most fun I've had in years.

I believe having an enthusiasm for what I do sets me apart from the crowd. I've spent years developing my marketing and social media skills. Combining my degree in Consumer Sciences and Marketing with my new technical education will make me a coveted employee within the tech industry.
